Source material for Alex's LinkedIn post. Not a draft of the post itself — these are extracts and notes Alex will draw from to write in his own voice.

## Format note for Alex

LinkedIn doesn't render fenced code blocks well. For the post itself:
- Use plain prose for technique descriptions
- Take screenshots of code/disasm and embed as images (much better engagement than text)
- Keep the post 800-1500 words; LinkedIn ~truncates~ at ~210 chars unless reader clicks "see more"
- Lead with a hook in the first 2 lines (those are always visible). Suggested: a single concrete moment from one challenge

The `highlights/` files contain BOTH the technical truth (for accuracy / your own reference) and the plain-prose summary (which you can adapt for the post). Pull what you want.
